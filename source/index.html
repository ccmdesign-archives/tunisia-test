Portal of Local Collectivities

The program of financing and evaluation of municipalities offers a budget for cities which fill the Minimum Obligatory Conditions.

The program of financing and evaluation of municipalities offers a budget for cities which fill the Minimum Obligatory Conditions. It also evaluates each city administration in terms of performance on Governance, Administration and Continuity. Here you can check the performance of your city and compare it with other ones.

The program exists in 2 parts: the first defines 5 Minimum Obligatory Conditions that, once met, give an additional budget to the city. The second part is a global evaluation of procedures that have taken place that increase participation, transparency and continuity of policies.

The Minimum Obligatory Conditions

These 5 points must be met in order to receive the financing. They reinforce the autonomy of the communities in order to execute their mandates and deliver local services while following principles of transparency, participation and responsibility towards the citizens.

Budget Approval

Approve of the provisional budget by the collectivity council within the stipulated date

Financial Authorization

Communicate the financial data of the previous year to the responsible authority

Annual Budget Approval

Approve of the Yearly Investment Program by the collectivity council through the participatory process

Publication

Publish the "Provisional Plan of Public Contracts" on the national site of Public Contracts

Final Approval

Approve the Responsibility Agreement between the collectivity and the state on the subvention benefits

The Evaluation System

Independent from the Minimal Obligatory Conditions, each community is also evaluated on 26 specific tasks. These tasks are classified in 3 categories: Governance, Administration and Continuity. Each task will ear the city points, that add up to a total of 100. These process are not related to the financing or to the Minimum Obligatory Conditions, nevertheless they show the level of performance of the city processes in terms of transparency, responsibility and participation towards the citizen.
